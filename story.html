Read the Story!

When Fa Zhou is called into war, Mulan steps up to taker her father's place. She dresses up as a soldier to join and fight in the military.

Throughout her journey, she faces multiple obstacles- but with the help of Li Shang, Mulan is able to overcome these challenges. The story highlights her strength and resilience as she navigates the environment.
